Donate Supplies

We accept the items listed below as donations from our community.

  • Socks - New, adult socks; men’s and women’s
  • Sleeping Bags - New and gently used (clean and undamaged); adult size only
  • Tents and Tarp - New and gently used (clean and undamaged); all sizes
  • Backpacks - New and gently used (clean and undamaged) hiking packs and backpacks; adult size only
  • Coats - New and gently used (clean and undamaged); adult size only. Hooded and rain repellent jackets especially useful
  • Hats and Gloves - New, adult hats and gloves

Please also see our Wish List, which reflects our most urgent needs.
If you purchase through Amazon, consider shopping on Amazon Smile.
